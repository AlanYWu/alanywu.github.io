---
title: "From one, many"
date: 2026-05-02
summary: "Why I keep coming back to emergence as the most interesting idea in science."
tags: ["essays", "emergence"]
---

A single neuron is not a brain. A single cell is not a body. A single rule
in Conway's game is not a glider. And yet, given enough copies, enough time,
and the right local interactions, the small thing becomes the larger thing,
and the larger thing has properties the small thing never had.

This is the quietest miracle I know. This site is a place to think about it.
