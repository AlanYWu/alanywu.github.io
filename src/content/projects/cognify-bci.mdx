Co-founder. Cognify builds non-invasive brain-computer interfaces so that
people with severe mobility impairments can control devices directly from
intention.

- 95% classification accuracy on SSVEP
- 81.25% control accuracy on motor imagery for hands-free VR
- Engineering a next-generation EEG device with sub-1mm² electrodes to
  replace bulky, invasive implants

Awarded \$1,500 at a Beijing startup competition. Member of the SSG Accelerator,
mentored by the Polsky Center at the University of Chicago.

A small intentional signal becoming a full physical action: emergence in the
most direct sense.
