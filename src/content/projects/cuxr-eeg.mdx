Project lead on the EEG / XR team at Cornell XR (CUXR). We're building the
plumbing that lets a small EEG signal drive interaction inside an immersive
environment, so that someone who can't move a controller can still author
inside it.

Cross-team work across hardware, software, and design subteams.
