Selected for the Tencent-funded *Aspiring Researcher in Science Project*
(~6% acceptance). The work paired aggressive feature engineering on public
seizure datasets with a portable EEG recording device, so the prediction
pipeline could leave the lab and run on a person walking around.

- 100% accuracy on the Bonn dataset after iterative feature design
- Portable hardware to support seizure warning in ambulatory use

Published article: [doi.org/10.54254/2753-8818/32/20240806](https://doi.org/10.54254/2753-8818/32/20240806).
